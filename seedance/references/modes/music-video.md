# Mode: music-video — Music Video & Beat-Synced Visual

Generate music video and beat-synced visual content prompts with @audio1 upload, timing markers, genre aesthetics, and performance choreography.

## Process

1. Collect context: genre (hip-hop, EDM, lo-fi, pop, rock, R&B, electronic), track BPM/energy, artist/performance or abstract visuals, duration, platform
2. Read `~/.claude/skills/seedance-music-video/references/details.md` for full beat-sync hook library, genre aesthetic guide, and performance movement vocabulary
3. Include `@audio1` reference in prompt — upload track so Seedance auto-syncs visuals to beat
4. Apply 2-second music hook matching genre: beat drop explosion, bass hit flash cut, lyric-on-screen reveal, performer entry with energy, abstract particle burst on intro
5. Specify timing markers in the prompt: "at 0:15 when the snare hits", "on the drop at 0:45", "bass breakdown from 1:20-1:50"
6. Describe beat-specific visuals: "flash cut on every kick drum", "360° rotate on the snare", "particle explosion on the drop"
7. Choose visual strategy: performance (artist in frame) vs abstract (no performer, pure visuals) vs narrative (story-driven)
8. Apply genre color aesthetic from details.md: hip-hop urban palette, EDM neon/black, lo-fi warm vintage, etc.
9. Describe camera choreography synced to song structure: verse (intimate), pre-chorus (build), chorus (wide/explosive)
10. Build complete prompt: @audio1 + genre + hook + timing markers + beat-specific visuals + camera choreography
11. Verify: @audio1 included, at least 3 timing markers specified, genre color aesthetic declared

## Notes
- @audio1 upload is the key differentiator — always include it for music video mode
- Extended guide: `~/.claude/skills/seedance-music-video/references/details.md`
