# Mode: fight-scenes — Fight Scene & Action Sequence

Generate fight scene and action sequence prompts: choreography description, physics language, multi-character staging, impact moments.

## Process

1. Collect context: fight style (martial arts, street brawl, sword combat, superhero, sci-fi), tone (realistic, stylized, anime-influenced), duration, platform ratio
2. Read `~/.claude/skills/seedance-fight-scenes/references/details.md` for full hook library (10+), combat vocabulary, environmental interaction guide
3. Apply 2-second hook for action: mid-action freeze frame, weapon clash with sparks, character charging camera, slow-motion impact, shockwave ground slam, blade unsheathing
4. Describe fight choreography: attacker/defender positioning, strike type, movement trajectory, spacing
5. Specify physics interactions: impact debris, dust clouds, environmental destruction, weapon momentum/follow-through
6. Describe lighting during action: impact flashes, muzzle flares, elemental effects casting shadows
7. Specify camera behavior: tracking shot following action, Dutch angle for disorientation, close insert for impact detail
8. Note depth of field: foreground action sharp, background blur during fast movement
9. Avoid: hyperrealistic gore (describe impact/aftermath), very quick multi-cuts (use longer dynamic takes instead)
10. Build complete prompt: hook + choreography + physics + lighting + camera + pacing
11. Verify: action is clearly described spatially, camera tracks the action, no gore requests

## Notes
- Longer takes with dynamic camera work better than rapid cuts on Seedance
