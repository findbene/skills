# Mode: real-estate — Real Estate & Architecture Showcase

Generate real estate, architecture, and interior design showcase video prompts: property walkthroughs, lighting times of day, immersive continuous tours.

## Process

1. Collect context: property type (luxury home, apartment, office, commercial, historic), price tier, key selling features, duration (30–120s), platform
2. Read `~/.claude/skills/seedance-real-estate/references/details.md` for full hook library (10+), camera movement sequences for real estate, lighting time-of-day guide, and walkthrough structure templates
3. Apply 2-second hook for real estate: drone aerial swoop toward property, door opening to reveal stunning interior, golden hour facade glow, underwater pool reveal, architectural detail extreme close-up that pulls back
4. Select lighting time-of-day matching property story: golden hour (aspirational warmth), twilight/blue hour (luxury drama), bright daylight (transparent/honest), overcast (even architectural clarity)
5. Describe camera path as a continuous flowing journey: exterior approach → entry → living spaces → focal rooms → outdoor spaces
6. Specify camera movement vocabulary: Steadicam walk, drone aerial, crane rise, dolly through doorway, gimbal orbit around feature
7. Include atmospheric elements where appropriate: light rays through windows, subtle dust particles in golden light, reflections in pool/glass
8. Describe materials and finishes: marble grain, hardwood texture, concrete finish, glass reflection behavior — these signal quality
9. Build complete prompt: hook + time-of-day + camera path + camera movements + materials + atmosphere
10. Verify: camera path flows logically through property, luxury signals are present, duration appropriate for property complexity

## Notes
- Continuous immersive flow (not cut-heavy) is Seedance's real-estate strength — describe the journey, not individual shots
