# Mode: anime-action — Anime-Style Video

Generate anime-style video prompts with cel shading, genre-specific aesthetics, effects library, and character animation.

## Process

1. Collect context: anime sub-genre (shonen, shojo, mecha, isekai, slice-of-life, horror, etc.), subject, duration, platform ratio
2. Read `~/.claude/skills/seedance-anime-action/references/details.md` for full genre guide (11 genres), effects library (23 entries), and technique encyclopedia
3. Select aesthetic philosophy: cel shading vs semi-realistic, line weight, color saturation level
4. Apply 2-second hook appropriate to genre (speed lines, dramatic freeze, transformation sequence, power-up glow)
5. Specify character design vocabulary: hair style, eye design, outfit, proportions
6. Describe camera techniques: dynamic angle, Dutch tilt, tracking shot style
7. Add effects: speed lines, impact frames, particle effects, screen-shaking, aura/energy effects from effects library
8. Build complete prompt with genre, aesthetic, hook, effects, and camera in sequence
9. Verify: genre vocabulary used, no live-action terms mixed in, duration matches energy level
