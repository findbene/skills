# Mode: brand-story — Brand Storytelling & Narrative Video

Generate brand narrative video prompts: story arcs, emotional registers, visual metaphors, voiceover timing, color/mood mapping.

## Process

1. Collect context: brand identity (industry, values, tone), narrative goal (origin story, mission, product launch, customer journey), duration, platform
2. Read `~/.claude/skills/seedance-brand-story/references/details.md` for full narrative arc library (10 arcs), hook techniques (12), industry-specific approaches, and emotional register guide
3. Select narrative arc matching brand goal (Hero's Journey, Before/After, Problem-Solution, Aspiration, etc.)
4. Apply 2-second hook: visual metaphor reveal, brand symbol animation, emotional contrast, or transformation sequence
5. Define emotional register: inspirational · authentic · urgent · serene · triumphant
6. Map color/mood: select from color palette guide in details.md matching brand values
7. Include voiceover/text timing markers if narration is planned
8. Build complete prompt: hook + narrative arc + emotional beats + visual metaphor + color direction
9. Verify: narrative arc is clear, brand voice is consistent, duration matches platform expectations

## Notes
- Visual metaphors are the differentiator — use the metaphor library in details.md
- Extended guide: `~/.claude/skills/seedance-brand-story/references/details.md`
