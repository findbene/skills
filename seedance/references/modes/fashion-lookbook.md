# Mode: fashion-lookbook — Fashion Lookbook & Model Showcase

Generate fashion lookbook and model showcase video prompts: garment motion, editorial hooks, fabric dynamics, color grading for fashion.

## Process

1. Collect context: garment type (haute couture, streetwear, athleisure, formal), target brand energy (luxury, editorial, commercial, campaign), duration, platform ratio
2. Read `~/.claude/skills/seedance-fashion-lookbook/references/details.md` for full hook library (10+), color grading presets for fashion, movement choreography guide
3. Apply 2-second hook matching brand energy: outfit reveal (curtain/door), power-walk, rapid flash cuts, fabric texture macro, slow-motion wind, mirror reveal
4. Describe garment motion: fabric drape physics, garment response to movement (billowing, stretching, flowing)
5. Specify model movement: walk style (confident stride, editorial pace, relaxed lifestyle), camera relationship
6. Define lighting: natural vs studio, golden hour vs high-key, dramatic shadows vs soft editorial
7. Apply fashion color grading: warm editorial, cool editorial, high-contrast fashion, brand signature palette
8. Specify aspect ratio: 9:16 for TikTok/Reels, 16:9 for YouTube fashion, 1:1 for Instagram feed
9. Build complete prompt: hook + garment + model motion + lighting + color grade + ratio
10. Verify: brand energy is consistent throughout, garment is always visible and the visual hero

## Notes
- Fabric motion clarity is Seedance's key strength for fashion — always specify drape behavior
