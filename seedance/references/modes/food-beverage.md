# Mode: food-beverage — Food & Beverage Video

Generate food and beverage video prompts: texture revelation, ASMR appetite triggers, lighting for food, slow-motion macro shots.

## Process

1. Collect context: food/drink category, hero shot goal (appetite trigger, brand showcase, recipe reveal), platform, duration (15–30s typical)
2. Read `~/.claude/skills/seedance-food-beverage/references/details.md` for full hook library (10+), lighting setups for food, and prompt template library
3. Apply 2-second food hook: cheese pull/stretch, slow-motion sauce pour, steam rising, knife crunch cut, chocolate break, ice cream melt, sizzle on hot surface, carbonation bubbles, ingredient cascade
4. Specify texture revelation: what tactile quality should dominate (crust crunch, ooze, glisten, steam, foam, drip)?
5. Describe lighting: backlight for steam/bubbles, golden key light for warm dishes, cool light for fresh/raw ingredients, overhead for overhead shots
6. Define camera movement: ultra-close macro → pull back to reveal full dish, or overhead pour shot, or tracking around the plate
7. Specify color grading: warm golden for comfort food, cool fresh for salads/drinks, vibrant saturated for social content
8. Include motion timing: slow-motion for money shots, real-time for sizzle/pour builds
9. Build complete prompt: hook + texture + lighting + camera + color grade + timing
10. Verify: the appetite trigger is in the first 2 seconds, food is the visual hero, lighting enhances appetite appeal

## Notes
- Sound implication matters even in silent preview — describe what a frame "sounds like" to guide motion pacing
